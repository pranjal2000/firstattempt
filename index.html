ME !

Hello everyone! I am Pranjal Singh, a student of IIT Delhi, currently pursuing my B Tech from Biochemical engineering and Biotechnology.Let me tell you a little about myself. My hometown is Gwalior (m.p.) and i did my schooling till class 10th from Gwalior Glory High School.I took science as my stream in class 11th and hence started preparing for JEE'18 then onwards. I joined Shikhar coaching classes in gwalior which had such a great environment to study. Teachers so caring and supportive , it just felt like home.I used to spent my entire day in the coaching premises, staying back after the classes. I went home only to sleep and next morning the cycle continued.Those were some best days of my life. I completely dedicate my achievement of getting admission into IITD to my teachers and my parents. Here I am today, in IITD , one of the most prestigious institution in the country. My experience till now in the college has been a mixed one .There were ups and downs but it has been a thrilling semester in all. I have developed interest in coding in past few months and have decided to learn more in the same field.

So why dev club?




My interests grew in coding and stuff after jee , when I stared to learn C++ on my own but never did a very thorough job. After encountering col100 as a course in first semester my interest in the field grew even more and i started liking the assignments the course offered and enjoyed doing them. I never really got enough time to learn a little more on my own . I know that i have a deep interest in this field and so i wanted to sharpen up my skills to be of some good use. I see devclub as a great opportunity for my self to improve my skills and learn new things in different aspects of this field. This is the most apt club for a person like me, I guess.
